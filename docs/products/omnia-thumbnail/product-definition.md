# Omnia Thumbnail — Product Definition

## Product Summary
Omnia Thumbnail is a lightweight thumbnail planning and optimization utility in OmniaTools for creator workflows.

The product focus is fast decision support for thumbnail clarity and presentation readiness.

## Problem
Creators frequently iterate thumbnails late in the workflow and need quick guidance to improve clarity, focus, and click-worthiness.

## Target Users
- Video/content creators preparing thumbnail concepts
- Small teams reviewing thumbnail options before publishing
- Creator assistants responsible for packaging content visuals

## Core Value Proposition
- Provide practical guidance for stronger thumbnail decisions
- Help creators evaluate readability and focal strength quickly
- Support repeatable pre-publish thumbnail checks

## Design Principles
- Keep scope narrow and execution fast
- Prioritize practical recommendations over heavy tooling
- Make outputs understandable to non-design specialists
- Preserve creator control in final visual choices

## Non-Goals (Initial Product)
- Full graphic design/canvas editor replacement
- Asset library/DAM system
- Deep A/B experiment infrastructure
- End-to-end content publishing suite

## Success Signals
- Users complete thumbnail review/planning steps faster
- Users can identify clear improvement actions before publish
- Users repeatedly use the tool during packaging workflows
